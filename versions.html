1.9.1 es una actualización menor para Minecraft la cual salio a mediados de marzo de 2016. 1.9.1 es compatible con todos los servidores 1.x.

The sound effect for the elytra was added: Item.elytra.flying sound event.

Attributes for the armor: generic.armorToughness The formula is: damage = damage x (1-max(Defense points/5, Defense points-damage/(2+Resistance/4))/25), so more Resistance will mean less reduction of the armor's defense points after very strong attacks.

Diamond armor has resistance 2, other armors have resistance 0. A full set of diamond armor will mean an increase of the resistance attribute up to 8.

New enableWeakAttacks option in options.txt Changing the property to False will disable the attack delay.
