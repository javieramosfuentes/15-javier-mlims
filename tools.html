Tools

There are many type of tools in minecraft that allows the players farm materials or attach mobs to gain HP, but each one has an interesting things about it. Here we can see all of these:

The enchantments can be used in certain tools such as the pickaxe, the sword, the boots, etc.
Repair All the tools with this enchantment repairs their HP when the player reaches experience
Fire aspect Fire Aspect adds 80 fire-ticks (4 seconds of burning) per level to the target. Because the first hit is caused by the item with this enchantment
Attraction Attraction allows the fish to take the hook faster when fishing
Swag Loot (also known as Loot) is an enchantment for swords that can cause creatures to drop more items and increase the chance of rare drops
Conductivity Conductivity (Channeling in the original version of the game) is a trident enchantment that creates a lightning bolt when used
Efficiency Efficiency (Efficiency in the original version of the game) is an enchantment that increases the player's breaking speed.
Impaling Impalement is an enchantment for a trident, which causes the trident to deal extra damage on each hit against aquatic creatures
Knockback Enchantment that we can apply on a sword to make our enemies, such as zombies, retreat more space when we hit them
Sharpness Sharpness (Sharpness in the original version of the game) is a sword or axe enchantment that increases melee damage
Sweeping edge Increases the damage you deal on mobs of enemies with a sweeping attack
Fortune Fortuna is an enchantment applied to digging and chopping tools that increases the amount and/or probability of obtaining the specific object
