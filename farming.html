Pickaxe

The pickaxe is one of the most used tools in the game for digging all minerals and some other types of blocks. To obtain resources from certain ores you need a pickaxe of a certain quality.

Fishing rod

The fishing rod is an object used for fishing in water. A common fishing without an enchanted rod can last between 25 and 10 seconds. You can fish a enchanted books and apply them to yours tools.

Shovel

Shovels are tools used to ease the process of collecting dirt, sand and other blocks with silk touch mining the complete block. Each type of shovel allows you to excavate faster, the netherite shovel is the best.

Hoe

A hoe is a tool used for tilling soil and sod on farmland, as well as for quickly harvesting certain vegetable blocks. This item is used in farms to harvesting the crop faster than if you do it with the hands.
